How is cyberbullying a major problem?

Cyberbullying is a big problem, and 60% of teenagers report that they have experienced some sort cyberbullying. Cyberbullying could cause disorders that could last a lifetime, such as social anxiety, starting to abuse on alcohol and drugs, and even eating disorder.


Girl being cyberbullied online.

According to the Statista Research Department, the number of cyberbullying incidents among students reported by schools in Japan from 2011 to 2019 has been increasing rapidly in recent years, going from 2992 incidents reported in 2011 to 17924 incidents reported in 2019.
